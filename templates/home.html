What is Kangen Water?

The term "Kangen" translates to "return to origin" in Japanese. Kangen Water® refers to alkaline water created using Enagic's advanced water filtration and ionisation systems. For over 50 years, it has been utilised in Japan to support the body's natural alkaline balance.

These devices convert regular tap water into refreshing and health-promoting alkaline water. Kangen Water® is often regarded as a healthier alternative to standard tap or purified water.
